Operations · Uganda

Where soil becomes credit.

Mavuno is the agricultural operating system for African smallholder farms. A yield-probability score, calibrated on seven live soil signals, replaces collateral as the gateway to irrigation finance via GPS-locked Energy Credits. On the same USSD session, the Community Resource Platform delivers live market prices, auto-matches farmers with institutional buyers, and provides AI-driven agronomy advice.
